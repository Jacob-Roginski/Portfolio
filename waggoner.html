Waggoner Financial

UI/UX Designer & Product Manager | Fall 2025

Overview

A comprehensive tool designed to support employees in their daily workflow by making essential information instantly accessible and ensuring new, role-specific updates are always visible. This project demonstrates the intersection of user-centered design and product management, focusing on accessibility and usability.

The Challenge

Employees needed a centralized platform to access critical information without information overload. The main challenges were:

  • Managing diverse user roles with different information needs
  • Ensuring updates are prominent but not intrusive
  • Creating an intuitive interface for varying technical proficiency
  • Maintaining accessibility standards across all features

My Approach

Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews and competitive analysis to understand pain points and market solutions.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Created low and high-fidelity wireframes in Figma, iterating based on stakeholder feedback.

Design Systems

Developed a comprehensive design system ensuring consistency and scalability across the platform.

User Testing

Validated design decisions through iterative user testing and accessibility audits.

Key Features

Role-Based Dashboard

Personalized information view tailored to each user's role and responsibilities.

Real-Time Updates

Instant notifications for role-specific updates without disrupting workflow.

Accessible Design

WCAG 2.1 AA compliant interface ensuring usability for all employees.

Intuitive Navigation

Clear information hierarchy and navigation patterns for quick access to needed data.

Outcome & Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of understanding diverse user needs and the value of iterative design. By focusing on accessibility from the start, we created a solution that works well for everyone. The role-based approach ensures that each user only sees information relevant to them, reducing cognitive load and improving overall efficiency.

Key learnings included the balance between feature richness and simplicity, the power of strong design systems for scalability, and how effective communication between design and product management drives better outcomes.
